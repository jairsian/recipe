Miso Glazed Steak Bowls with Creamy Cucumber Salad & Sticky Rice

A quick steak and rice bowl with sticky rice, miso-glazed steak, and a creamy cucumber salad with tahini, lime, and spring onion.

By emthenutritionist

Yield: 1 serving

Calories: 560 kcal

Protein: 45 g

Ingredients

  • 160g lean steak, such as rump or fillet
  • 1 tsp miso
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp yoghurt
  • 1 heaped tsp tahini
  • 1 tsp miso
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 spring onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, chopped
  • 40g sticky rice, dry weight
  • 1 lime wedge, to serve
  • Chilli flakes, to serve

Instructions

  1. Mix together the miso, honey, soy sauce, garlic paste and sesame oil to make the glaze, then set aside.
  2. Cook the sticky rice according to the packet instructions.
  3. Heat a frying pan over a high heat. Sear the steak on both sides until nicely coloured.
  4. Flip the steak, pour over the glaze and let it bubble away, turning the steak every 30 seconds or so until the sauce has thickened and glazed the meat.
  5. Cook to your preferred doneness, then remove from the pan and leave to rest for a few minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, stir together the yoghurt, tahini, miso, honey, lime zest and juice, garlic paste and spring onion until smooth.
  7. Toss the chopped cucumber through the dressing.
  8. Spoon the rice into your bowl, add the creamy cucumber salad, then slice the steak and arrange over the top.
  9. Spoon over any extra glaze from the pan and finish with a wedge of lime and a pinch of chilli flakes.
